Childhood Bedroom

My childhood bedroom was a small, cozy space that reflected my personality and interests. The walls were painted a soft pastel color, adorned with posters of my favorite bands and movie characters. A twin bed with a colorful quilt sat against one wall, while a small desk cluttered with books and art supplies occupied another corner. Shelves lined with toys, board games, and cherished mementos added a touch of nostalgia to the room. It was a sanctuary where I spent countless hours reading, drawing, and dreaming about the future.
